About Us

Transmission from the year 2187

Wait... How Did You Get Here?

Congratulations, time traveler! You've stumbled upon Fosterly — the galaxy's most questionably-sourced e-commerce platform, beamed back from the year 2187. We were supposed to launch in the Andromeda sector, but someone spilled coffee on the deployment server and, well... here we are. On your internet. In your timeline. Our bad.

Our "Mission"

To provide you with products that definitely exist and are absolutely not placeholder items from a developer's imagination. Every product in our catalog has been personally approved by a sentient AI named Gerald. Gerald has questionable taste, but impeccable algorithms.

The Team

We are a crack team of exactly one developer and an unreasonable number of Stack Overflow tabs. Our CEO is also the intern. The marketing department is a cron job that tweets motivational quotes at 3 AM. HR is a rubber duck on a desk somewhere in Texas.

Security & Trust

Your data is protected by military-grade encryption and also by the fact that we don't actually store anything important. Our security audit was conducted by Gerald (the AI, remember?) who gave us a rating of "yeah, looks fine I guess." We sleep soundly at night, mostly because of the melatonin.

Why "Fosterly"?

Great question. In the future, all company names are generated by throwing Scrabble tiles at a neural network. "Fosterly" beat out strong contenders like "Blorpify," "Yeetonomics," and "ShopShopBabyShop." We think we made the right call. The domain was also $12, which helped.

But Seriously...

This is a portfolio project built by Clark Foster to showcase full-stack development skills. It features Angular, Spring Boot, a cyber-punk design theme, and more caffeine than any human should legally consume. Thanks for stopping by!
