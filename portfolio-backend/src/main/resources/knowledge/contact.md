# Contact

The Contact page (`/contact`) on this site is the primary way to reach Clark.
It posts to a rate-limited Spring Boot endpoint that delivers email through
the configured SMTP provider.

## Channels

- **Contact form**: `/contact` on this site.
- **Email (project routing)**: messages submitted via the form are delivered
  to the address configured in the `CONTACT_EMAIL` environment variable
  (default `clark@clarkfoster.com`).
- **GitHub**: [github.com/clark22134](https://github.com/clark22134)
- **LinkedIn**: linked from the Contact page.

## What to include

- A short description of the role, project, or question.
- Timeline expectations.
- Any links (job posting, repo, design doc) you would like Clark to look at.

## Response time

Clark aims to respond to recruiter and hiring-manager messages within one
business day.
